![Pengaturan diperpanjang](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ar135ta/alltv/main/TUTOR%20OTT/No.2.png)

![Cadangkan dan pulihkan](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ar135ta/alltv/main/TUTOR%20OTT/No.3.png)
